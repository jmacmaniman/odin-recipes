Lupini Bean Seitan

Back to Master Recipe List

Description

My daily breakfast after coffee, typically mid-morning. Simple and quick to make!

Macros: 60g Protein / 60g Carbs / 13g Fat

Ingredients

  • 480g Soy Milk
  • 130g Frozen Wild Blueberries
  • 1 Banana
  • 66g Vega Chocolate Protein Powder
  • 2tsp Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add the blueberries and then banana to blender cup.
  2. Add milk
  3. Add protein powder
  4. Add Cinnamon
  5. Blend for ~20 seconds until fully mixed
